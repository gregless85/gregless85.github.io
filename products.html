The clear blue sky is significantly tied to cricket in Australia. It means the game is ready to go, the spectators are ready for a days action. It is also synonymous with the sun, the huge burning ball in the sky giving us warmth light and unfortunately UV rays. Blue Sky Zinc uses ultramarine oxides another inorganic metal, to give life to your face. All oxides at Cover Zinc are carefully sourced to ensure only the best products with the best manufacturing processes are created. Blue Sky Zinc will not run or leak colour onto your face, and will provide excellent sun protection for hours while you spend time under the blue sky.

Drawing its colour inspiration from traditional aboriginal paintings to look natural and identify with Australian culture. The aboriginals used red ochre to create inspiring paintings, to honour the fallen and as body decoration during times of celebration. Know as Iron Oxide in the scientific world, red ochre is added to the zinc to really make the colour stand out. Red Ochre is safe to apply to the skin and is another stable mineral keeping in line with the ethos of safe, environmentally products produced here at Cover Zinc

The day after after a big game what better way to relax than head to the beach and spend some time in the ocean resting sore muscles. Ocean Green takes its inspiration from the down days, those spend relaxing in the field, by a lake or in the sea. It uses hydrated green oxide to colour, which is often used in soaps. It will not run or leak and looks great. Cover Zinc believes in down days while maintaining excellent protection for your skin. Ocean Green is the perfect colour to show you are just taking it easy today.
